Parking
Hampton Inn/Home2 [Link to CCC Hotels Page here]
Parking is available onsite for cruisers staying at one of the Colossalcon Cruise's official hotels. It is $10/night while you have your hotel reservation and then $25/night for up to 7 days if you do not have an active hotel reservation.
Other popular parking options are:
Port Parking Space: https://portparkingspace.com/
Parking here is off-site and accessible via shuttle. Parking is approximately $15/day after fees and taxes. Shuttle accommodates multiple passengers from the same vehicle.
Port Canaveral Terminal Parking: https://www.portcanaveral.com/Cruise/Directions-Parking
Parking here is on-site and within walking distance of the terminal. Parking is approximately $17/Day + tax including arrival and departure day. This is Credit card only. There are disabled spaces available for no fee, however, there is a criteria list that must be met to utilize this for no fee. Information regarding this is available on the website listed above.
No reservations are needed to utilize this.
