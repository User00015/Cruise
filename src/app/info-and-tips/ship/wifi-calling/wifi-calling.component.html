Tip: Wi-Fi Calling
January 27, 2024
Leverage Wi-Fi calling onboard for enhanced communication, bypassing the limitations of messenger apps and cellular services.
About Wi-Fi Calling
Wi-Fi calling uses the ship’s Wi-Fi network for phone calls, offering clearer quality and seamless use of your existing phone number. This feature is ideal for staying in touch with others onboard and onshore without incurring additional costs.
Steps to Enable
  • Check your phone’s compatibility.
  • Enable Wi-Fi calling in your device settings.
  • Connect to the ship’s Wi-Fi.
Ensure you’re aware of any potential charges by your service provider, but generally, Wi-Fi calling is a cost-effective option for cruisers.
By opting for Wi-Fi calling, you ensure a more reliable and cost-efficient way to communicate while enjoying your cruise.
