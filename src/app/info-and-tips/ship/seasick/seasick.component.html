The Ship
Tips: Seasickness
January 27, 2024
Are you feeling a bit seasick?
There is a medical center onboard that can provide you with free Dramamine. Typically the medical center is located on Deck 1 rear of the boat. Plan ahead and take it at the first sign of unease since it can take a while to take effect.
Please note, it is not advised to drink alcohol and take Dramamine.
