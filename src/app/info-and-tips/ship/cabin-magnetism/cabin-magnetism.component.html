The Ship
Tip: Cabin Magnetism
January 27, 2024
Transform your cruise cabin space with the magic of magnetism. Utilize magnets to organize and personalize your living area effectively.
Magnetic Surfaces
  • Walls & Doors:
    Discover that cabin walls and doors are made of ferrous metal, making them perfect surfaces for magnetic attachments.
Creative Uses
  • Organization
    Use magnets to hang important documents, daily planners, or photos, keeping your space tidy and items easily accessible.
  • Decoration:
    Personalize your door or walls with magnetic decorations, like a dry erase board for notes and drawings, adding a touch of home to your cabin.
  • Pre-Purchase Benefits:
    Purchasing your drink package before boarding can offer savings compared to buying on the ship.
