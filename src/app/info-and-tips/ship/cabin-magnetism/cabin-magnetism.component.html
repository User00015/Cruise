Tips: Cabin Tips
January 27, 2024
Did you know that the walls, main door, and ceiling of your cabin are all magnetic?
This means you can print things like magnetic hooks or decorations to personalize and organize your cabin.
Things like
  • Magnetic hooks
  • Dry-erase boards
  • Door decorations
Mattress Comfort
Do you find the mattress too firm? Ask your room steward for a foam topper for added softness. This can be in limited supply so you can always bring your own. A compact solution is an inflatable camping mat under the sheets.
Balcony Privacy
Balconies offer limited privacy, so please be mindful of your surroundings.
Safes
In every room, there is a free-to-use safe for your valuables. It is compact but secure. Instructions for use are located on the safe door.
Bathrooms
Standard bathrooms are compact. An airfreshener might be a usedful addition to your packing list.
