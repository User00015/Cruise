The Ship
Tips: Main Dining
January 27, 2024
This section covers how table assignment in the main dining room works.
Table Assignment
  • Notification:
    Your table assignment will be available in the cruise app shortly before your cruise begins.
  • Flexibility:
    If you wish to change your table or dining time, contact customer service. They’ll assist with available options.
Ordering Flexibility
  • You’re not limited to a single appetizer, entrée, and dessert. Feel free to order multiple dishes at once if you’re extra hungry. Just remember to place all your orders together to streamline service.
Drink Package Usage
  • Your drink package is valid in the Main Dining Room. This means you can enjoy a wide range of beverages, from soft drinks to cocktails, as part of your package. Availability may vary, so check the specific terms of your package.
