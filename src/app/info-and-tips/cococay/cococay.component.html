Coco-Cay
Info: Events on the island
January 27, 2024
This section covers common places to visit and events to do on Coco-Cay
Places to eat.
All food on the island is complimentary, and covered by your cruise.
  • Skipper's Grill:
    All american food and ice cream.
  • Chill Grill:
    Classic BBQ
  • Taco Bar:
    Build your own tacos.
  • Snack Shack:
    Specialty chicken sandwiches.
  • Buffet:
    A buffet is also available for a certain amount of time. Check the ship schedule.
Bars and pools
The following bars and pools are available.
  • Oasis lagoon swim-up bar:
    One of the primary swim-up bars colossal con goers attend. A very large swim up pool with submerged bar centered on the north end.
  • Captain Jack's:
    At the entrance to the island. Serves both food and drinks.
  • South Beach:
    Despite the name, this beach is located on the north end of the island. Includes games such as volleyball, the Snack Shack and drinks are available on a floating bar.
  • Chill Island:
    On the west end of the island. Includes the Chill Grill, white sand beaches and plenty of room to relax.
Thrill Waterpark
Coco-Cay also includes a separate waterpark.
  • Daredevil's Tower:
    One of two waterslides.
  • Wave Pool:
    A massive wave pool.
  • Splash Summit:
    Six different slides, including Daredevil's Peak
