Pre/Post Cruise
Info: Check-In Process
January 27, 2024
This will cover various issues that may occur prior to boarding.
  • Parking at the Port
    • Cost: Parking is available on-site at a rate of $25 per night. Payment is required upon arrival.
  • Baggage Handling
    • Porter Service: Upon arrival, you have the option to entrust your bags to an official Royal Caribbean Porter. While tipping is customary and appreciated, it’s optional.
    • Warning: Avoid giving porters bags containing essential items like medication or passports, as you won’t have access to these bags immediately.
    • Tip: For those preferring to keep their luggage with them, consider “The Key” upgrade for direct luggage delivery to your cabin.
  • Terminal Check-In
    • Documentation: Ensure you have your Set Sail QR Code (available in the Royal Caribbean App) and appropriate identification (Passport/Passport Card or Birth Certificate and Driver’s License).
  • Boarding Wait
    • Timing: Boarding typically commences between 10am and 11am, after the ship is cleared. Your Set Sail QR Code will be your main document for boarding.
  • Pre-Cabin Access
    • Onboard Purchases: Use your Set Sail QR Code for transactions onboard until your cabin becomes accessible, generally around 1-2pm.
